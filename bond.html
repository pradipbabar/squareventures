Ways to Invest in Bonds?

Bonds are a type of investment that results in an investor lending money to the bond issuer in exchange for interest payments.
How do Bonds work?
When an investor purchases a bond, they are "loaning" that money (called the principal) to the bond issuer, which is usually raising money for some project. When the bond matures, the issuer repays the principal to the investor
Why do Bonds matter?
Bonds and other fixed-income securities play a critical role in an investor's portfolio. Owning bonds helps to diversify a portfolio, as the bond market doesn't rise or fall alongside the stock market.
